# Daily Routine & Timeline

A structured schedule based on [Personal Growth](PERSONAL_GROTH.md) and [Simple Rules](SIMPLE_RULES.md).

## Morning: The Power Hour (Before Job)
- **05:00 AM:** Wake Up & Fajr Prayer
- **05:30 AM:** Morning Exercise & Meditation
- **06:00 AM:** **Deep Work Block 1: Problem Solving & DSA** (~1.5 hours)
- **07:30 AM:** Breakfast & Prep for Work
- **08:15 AM:** Commute (Audiobooks / Technical Podcasts)

## Day: Professional Focus (9 - 5)
- **09:00 AM:** **Main Job Hours** (Focus on high performance)
- **01:15 PM:** Zuhr Prayer & Lunch (Quick LinkedIn check/post)
- **05:00 PM:** End of Work & Commute (Decompression / Audiobooks)

## Evening: Growth & Family (After Job)
- **06:00 PM:** Maghrib Prayer & Family Time (Protected)
- **07:30 PM:** **Deep Work Block 2: Core Learning** (Flutter, Architecture, AI) (~2 hours)
- **09:30 PM:** Isha Prayer & Late Dinner
- **10:15 PM:** Journaling & Reflection (Review the day)
- **10:30 PM:** Sleep (Essential for 5 AM wake up)

> [!TIP]
> Use your lunch break for "low energy" tasks like LinkedIn engagement or technical reading so your evening Deep Work is focused strictly on coding/learning.

> [!NOTE]
> Consistency over intensity. Small daily actions compound into mastery.
