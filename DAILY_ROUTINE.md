# Daily Routine & Timeline

A structured schedule based on [Personal Growth](PERSONAL_GROTH.md) and [Simple Rules](SIMPLE_RULES.md).

## Morning: Spiritual & Physical Foundation
- **05:00 AM:** Wake Up & Fajr Prayer (Rule: Do not skip namaz)
- **05:30 AM:** Morning Exercise & Meditation (Rule: Exercise & Meditate Everyday)
- **06:30 AM:** **Deep Work Block 1: Problem Solving & DSA** (~1 hour)

## Day: Growth & Professional Development
- **07:30 AM:** Healthy Breakfast & Quick LinkedIn Post/Engagement
- **08:00 AM:** **Deep Work Block 2: Core Learning** (Flutter, Architecture, AI) (~2 hours)
- **10:00 AM:** Break / Technical Reading / Learning Exploration
- **01:15 PM:** Zuhr Prayer & Lunch
- **02:00 PM:** **Deep Work Block 3: Job Search / Opportunities / Project Work**
- **04:30 PM:** Asr Prayer & Short Walk / Reading (Audiobooks)

## Evening: Reflection & Family
- **06:00 PM:** Maghrib Prayer & Family Time (Rule: Spend time with family)
- **07:00 PM:** Learning / Documentation / Technical Writing (3 hours once a week)
- **08:30 PM:** Isha Prayer & Dinner
- **09:30 PM:** Journaling & Reflection (Review goals and habits)
- **10:00 PM:** Sleep early to repeat tomorrow.

> [!NOTE]
> Consistency over intensity. Small daily actions compound into mastery.
