# Learning & Professional Growth System

Comprehensive strategy for technical excellence and personal branding.

## Technical Skill Matrix
- **Core:** Flutter (Senior Level), Software Architecture.
- **Support:** Backend Basics, System Design.
- **Future:** AI (Prompt & Context Engineering).
- **Foundation:** DSA & Problem Solving (Daily deep work).

## LinkedIn Strategy
- **Daily Actions:** Predefined engagement and posting.
- **Branding:** SEO optimization and Technical Writing.
- **Networking:** Engage with industry leaders and recruiters.

## Deep Work Structure
- **Block 1 (1hr):** Hard Problem Solving (DSA).
- **Block 2 (2hr):** High-level technical topics / Project Architecture.
- **Weekly (3hr):** Technical Writing & Reflection.

## Job Hunt & Opportunities
- Dedicated time blocks for outreach and skill-matching.
- Focus on "Interview Fluency" through mock prep.
