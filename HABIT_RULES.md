# Habit & Rule System

The non-negotiables for a disciplined life.

## Spiritual Rules
- **5 Daily Prayers:** Never skip namaz.
- **Meditation:** Daily morning mindfulness sessions.

## Lifestyle Rules
- **Quit Bad Habits:** Identifying and eliminating friction.
- **Health:** Daily exercise and early wake-up.
- **Family:** Protected time for family every evening.

## Discipline & Self-Control
- Replace scrolling with books/audiobooks.
- Strict adherence to deep work blocks.
- Journaling every night to track progress.
